Since 2 April 2025, I have been issuing daily tornado outlooks for the United States, similar but seperate from the Storm Prediction Center. This is the website I have made for these outlooks, though it was only made months after I started drawing outlooks, & was only published here on Github on 5 March 2026, almost a full year after I started. <br>

Days where I did not draw an outlook are 27 June 2025, where I forgot; 3 & 7 August 2025, where I also forgot; 11 October 2025, where I probably also forgot; 8 November 2025, that time due to a power-outage, & most-recently 4 December 2025, where I once again forgot.
