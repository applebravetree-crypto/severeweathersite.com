2: Location

If a non-notable outlook extends into a region where it is—for example, if a 3/5 moderate-risk exists in eastern New-Mexico (Mountain East region; not notable), & the surrounding 2/5 elevated-risk extends into Arizona (Mountain West region; notable)—that outlook is still considered notable.
