Outlooks - Miscellaneous - Updates

Every morning, around 7:00 to 8:00 UTC on weekdays & 9:00 to 11:00 UTC on weekends (including Friday), I check SPC's High-Resolution Ensemble Forecast page & draw a map of where in Mexico, the Contiguous Unites States, southern Canada, & some surrounding areas, I think tornadoes &/or thunderstorms will be possible. These are called Tornado outlooks.

Tornado outlooks for tomorrow or overmorrow are also occasionally issued. Sometimes, I will check long-range forecast models like GFS & draw a tornado outlook for somewhere in the near future, almost always less than 10 days from issuance.

As much as I consider myself pretty good at forecasting tornadoes, I am NOT a METEOROLOGIST!!! Do not use this website for decision-making! For tornado outlooks made by actual professionals, visit The United States' official Storm Prediction Center.
